  • Types of Examinations:-

    Generally there are three types of &examination, first is the general examination conducted by various board across the country for the secondary and higher secondary levels. Second are the entrance exams which are conducted for securing admission in particular college or university. Third are the competitive exams for obtaining the job at specific segment in the country.

  • Entrance Examinations:-

    The main objective of placing the entrance exams in Indian education system is for providing and securing the admission in different colleges in India.It is based on the study curriculum in which student has studied earlier. Purpose of entrance exams is to test the ability of a student to handle the academics which he is going to handle in future and tells about the in-depth knowledge in particular field. Entrance exam include both descriptive and objective approach. Latest development in the system includes only objective question so that student can get rid of cramming.

  • Competitive Exams:-

    These types of examination are more of focusing on allotting a job opportunity to the students. Student can take up these exams only after the completion of any graduation degree or in the final year of completion. These exams are of the objective type only covering the various aspects of current market needs and wants. Competitive exam tries to pull out the best talent possible for a particular job profile. Curriculum of competitive exam generally includes reasoning ability, mathematics, data interpretation and general knowledge. It is the exam which s not confined to particular sector or faculty it test the overall ability and knowledge of the student.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How to register?

    Registration
    Click 'Register' on the top right corner of the page and you will see a registration form. This will take you hardly 2 minutes to fill up. Just submit your information, verify your mobile number and enjoy the various benefits of being a Guide member.

    Mobile number - Is it compulsory?

    Yes, mobile number is needed to verify and complete your registration and also ensure account safety.

    How do I edit my profile on Guide?

    Editing your Guide account profile information is very simple. Here's how to do it:

    Can I update the email id?

    Email id cannot be changed as it is mapped to your account at the time of registration. However, you can choose to add your alternate email id in your ‘Personal Information’ section. The other option is to simply create a new Guide account with your new email id that you wish to use.

    How do I change my password?

    To change your password, Go to your account homepage and click 'Settings'. Now you can change or replace your new password and save.

    How do I update my mobile number?

    Go to your ‘Profile’ page, click ‘Edit’ (top right corner) on the ‘Personal Information’ section and then change your new mobile number. Please remember to click ‘Save’.

    How do I update profile details?

    You can update your profile information divided into four sections – Personal Information, Educational Background, Work Experience (if applicable) and Communication Settings.

    How to update communication preferences?

    You can change or update your ‘Communication Preferences’ under ‘Settings’ to start or stop receiving email notifications from Guide. This is useful mail, especially delivered to your inbox to serve your information needs such as college application deadlines, exam dates, prep tips etc. based on your profile. In case you no longer need this service, you may uncheck the box under ‘Communication Preferences’.

    How do I update my profile photo?

    Update Photo option is seen if you point the mouse over your profile photo box. You can select this and upload your image.

    Is all information compulsory to create my profile?

    No. Apart from your full name, mobile no. and email id, the rest of the fields are optional. However, the page asks for only necessary information and adding these details will help create a complete profile page for you and help us cater to your information needs better.

    What privacy settings do I have for my Profile?

    You have adequate control on your Guide profile privacy. The fields that have a privacy icon (eye) next to them can be set on or off by you to show or hide those details from public view.

  • How do I search for colleges or courses?

    To search for a particular college or course, just enter its name in the search box on top of Guide homepage. As you start typing, you'll see a dropdown, you can select the name of your desired course/college from this menu as well.

    The other way is to search for colleges by categories - MBA, Engineering, Design and Other Courses - shown on the Top global navigation bar. There is an option to select Study Abroad as well. Under each of these respective categories, you can search - Popular Courses, Colleges by Location, Top Colleges and even Compare Colleges.
